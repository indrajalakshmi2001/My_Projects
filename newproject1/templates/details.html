Villa

  • A villa is a luxurious, standalone residence often characterized by spacious living areas, high-end amenities, and a private outdoor space, such as a garden or pool.

  • Typically located in scenic or exclusive areas, villas are designed to offer privacy and comfort, making them ideal for family living or as vacation homes.

  • They often feature elegant architecture, multiple bedrooms, and are equipped with modern conveniences to provide a sophisticated lifestyle.
